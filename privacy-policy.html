Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 19, 2026

SHARP Sports LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates the SHARP mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use SHARP.

By using SHARP, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and any display name you choose.
  • Profile information: Information you optionally provide, including your favorite sports teams, betting experience level, and avatar preferences.
  • Prediction data: The sports predictions you make within the App, including teams, predicted scores, confidence ratings, and outcomes.
  • Usage data: Basic information about how you interact with the App, such as which features you use and when.
  • Device information: Device type, operating system, and push notification tokens (if you enable notifications).
  • Purchase information: If you subscribe to SHARP Pro, transaction records are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. We do not store your payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the SHARP App
  • Track and display your prediction record and statistics
  • Send push notifications about your predicted games (if enabled)
  • Process and manage your SHARP Pro subscription
  • Respond to your support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraudulent or abusive activity

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

SHARP uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy practices:

  • Supabase — database and authentication provider. Your account data and predictions are stored on Supabase's servers. Supabase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — subscription and in-app purchase management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Expo / Expo Push Notifications — app delivery platform and push notification service. Expo Privacy Policy
  • ESPN API — public sports data (schedules, scores, team information). No personal data is shared with ESPN.
  • Apple App Store / Google Play — app distribution and payment processing. Subject to Apple's and Google's respective privacy policies.

4. Data Retention

We retain your account information and prediction history for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it for legal or compliance reasons.

5. Push Notifications

If you enable push notifications, we collect your device's push token to send you alerts about your predicted games (such as game start times and final results). You can disable notifications at any time in the App under More → Alerts & Notifications, or in your device's system settings.

6. Children's Privacy

SHARP is intended for users 21 years of age and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it promptly.

7. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information, including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and secure database storage through Supabase. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@sharpapp.io.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
